BIO...

Sheba W began her artistic journey through the medium of photography in Italy and Iceland. Over the years, she has worked extensively across various creative disciplines, including short and feature films, television, documentaries, and theatre. Her collaborations span multiple countries, with a primary focus in Iceland, Kenya, and Italy.
Sheba is the founder of Turpentine Laboratory and Sheba Wonder Production Ltd, two creative ventures dedicated to innovative artistic expression and cross-disciplinary storytelling. She currently lives and works in Nairobi, Kenya.
